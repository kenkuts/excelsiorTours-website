Kawasan Falls

        Kawasan Falls is located in a province of Cebu called Badian. Badian is located in the southern part of the island close to Moalboal. Kawasan has many waterfalls. The first one is the largest of the three, and is usually the most crowded. An uphill climb for fifteen minutes will take you to the second falls after that there is third and last waterfall. Its a good idea to bring in your own food, the resort’s restaurants are not cheap and prices are not marked.

        Cottages and tables are available for rent, cottages are priced from 1,500 to 2,500 pesos(about $35 - $55) and tables are 500 pesos (about $12), the rooms are also available for overnight stay. You can rent a raft for 300 pesos(about $8) to take you around the “pool” or for a refreshing “shower massage” under the falling water. Life vests are also available if needed.
