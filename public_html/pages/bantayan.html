Bantayan Island

        Bantayan is a first class municipality on Bantayan island in the province of Cebu. It is situated to the west of the northern end of Cebu Island. It is the island's most populous and largest town. Bantayan Island is a butterfly-shaped island in the Philippines. It has other numerous satellite and smaller islands that are best for snorkeling, diving, swimming or just relaxing. That means you need to be armed with snorkel gears, diving gears, fishing tools, among other fun sea water sports and activities.

        The beaches are clean and the water is amazing. You can scuba dive, swim, snorkel, kayak, boat, ski and any sort of fun water sports. Since the island offers so much, not only to locals but also to all visitors, more resorts are being established to cater the needs of the guests.
